Who is this for?

         
This workshop is designed for traditional artisans, handicraftsmen, weavers, bamboo workers and all such professionals who are looking for market.






          Student who is eager to learn our traditional art and looking for experienced instructor.





          Housewife who knows traditional art like bamboo mat making, basket making, weaving, casement designing etc. and wants to earn money sitting at home or wants to learn it from an experienced person.





          People who want to start their own startup and are looking for ideas, market knowledge or are startup owners looking for global markets, raw materials at cheap rates or experienced employees.





          Social workers who want to be a part of the Swadeshi movement and are looking for a platform.







What will you get from this workshop?


  • Business Ideas
  • How to take your product to the global market
  • How to get your art recognized globally
  • Information about the extinct arts of India and their revival
