ABOUT TRADE SENSE

Our mission is to promote and revive traditional art and to help the people associated with traditional arts to get fair price for their art work in market so that our traditional art will survive and also how to connect traditional arts with modernity today so that it gets a global identity.

What services do we provide?
  • we help traditional artisans, handicrafts men, weavers, bamboo workers and all such professionals who are looking for market.
  • we help Student who is eager to learn our traditional art and looking for experienced instructor.
  • we help Housewife who knows traditional art like bamboo mat making, basket making, weaving, casement designing etc. and wants to earn money sitting at home or wants to learn it from an experienced person.
  • we help the one who want to start their own startup and are looking for ideas, market knowledge or are startup owners looking for global markets, raw materials at cheap rates or experienced employees.
  • we help Social workers who want to be a part of the Swadeshi movement and are looking for a platform.
  • All other similar services.For more details you can inquire us.
